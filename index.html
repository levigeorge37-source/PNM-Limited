About Us

Connecting Kenyan Farms to Global Markets

Based in Kenya, PNM Limited works directly with farmers and aggregators to supply high-quality fruits and vegetables to global buyers. Our mission is to bridge the gap between local agricultural excellence and international market demands.

We understand the unique challenges of fresh produce export and have built a robust network that ensures quality, reliability, and timely delivery. From farm to port, we manage every step of the supply chain with precision and care.
